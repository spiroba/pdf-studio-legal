# Privacy Policy for PDF Studio

**Last Updated: November 16, 2025**

## Introduction

PDF Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our PDF Studio mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Documents and Files**: PDF files and images you create, import, or convert within the App
- **Signatures**: Digital signatures you create and save in the App
- **Settings and Preferences**: Your language preference, theme settings, and app configuration

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, conversion statistics, and app performance metrics
- **Region Information**: Country/region code for determining subscription availability (EU compliance)

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud synchronization:
- Your documents, folders, and app data are stored in your personal iCloud account
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy applies to synced data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide App Functionality**
   - Process PDF conversions and document operations
   - Save and manage your documents and signatures
   - Sync data across your devices via iCloud (if enabled)

2. **Improve User Experience**
   - Remember your preferences and settings
   - Provide localized content in your preferred language
   - Optimize app performance

3. **Subscription Management**
   - Process in-app purchases through Apple's App Store
   - Determine regional subscription availability
   - Manage free tier usage limits (outside EU)

4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with EU Digital Services Act (DSA) requirements
   - Provide free unlimited access to EU users as required by law

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All documents and files are stored **locally on your device**
- We do not upload your documents to our servers
- Your data remains under your control

### iCloud Storage (Optional)

- When enabled, data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
- We have no access to your iCloud data
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in Settings

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- Data encryption at rest and in transit
- Secure handling of in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit
- No collection of personally identifiable information

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services

The App uses the following Apple services:
- **App Store / StoreKit**: For in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **iCloud**: For optional data synchronization (user-controlled)

Apple's Privacy Policy applies to these services: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### No Third-Party Analytics

We do **not** use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: View all your documents and data within the App
- **Delete**: Delete individual documents or clear all app data
- **Export**: Share or export your documents at any time
- **iCloud Sync**: Enable or disable iCloud synchronization in Settings

### EU User Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

### Subscription Management

- Manage subscriptions through your Apple ID account settings
- Cancel subscriptions at any time without penalty
- EU users receive full app functionality for free

## Regional Features

### EU Users

In compliance with EU regulations:
- **No subscriptions required**: Full app functionality is free
- **No usage limits**: Unlimited conversions and documents
- **Data minimization**: We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality

### Non-EU Users

- **Free tier**: 3 conversions per day, up to 5 documents
- **Premium subscriptions**: Available for unlimited usage
- **Optional purchase**: All subscriptions are optional

## Children's Privacy

PDF Studio is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Displaying an in-app notification for material changes
- Posting the updated policy in the App

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Data Retention

- **Documents**: Stored on your device until you delete them
- **Preferences**: Retained until you uninstall the App or reset settings
- **iCloud Data**: Retained in your iCloud account according to your iCloud settings
- **Usage Statistics**: Stored locally and reset daily/upon app deletion

## International Data Transfers

Your data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not transfer your data internationally. If you use iCloud, Apple's data transfer policies apply.
